Sathiyan Gnanasekaran

As of November 2024, he is the highest ranked Indian men's singles player in the world at 74. He was the first Indian to break into the top 25 of the world rankings in 2019. He won a bronze medal at the 2018 Asian Games and a team gold medal at the 2018 Commonwealth Games. He also won three medals at the 2022 Commonwealth Games, including a gold in the men's team event.

Manika Batra

She was the first Indian woman to break into the top 25 of the world Women's singles rankings. She won a gold medal in the individual and team categories at the 2018 Commonwealth Games.
