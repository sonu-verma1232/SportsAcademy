PV Sindhu

Became the first Indian to win the badminton World Championships and the second to win consecutive Olympic medals. She won a silver at the 2016 Rio Olympics and a bronze at the 2020 Tokyo Olympics.

Lakshya Sen

Won his first BWF World Tour title at the 2019 Dutch Open. He went on to win back-to-back Super 100 tournaments, which helped him break into the top 50 of the badminton world rankings.
