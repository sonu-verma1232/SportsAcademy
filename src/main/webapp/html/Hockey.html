P.R Sreejesh

Sreejesh, a former Indian hockey goalkeeper and team captain, is considered one of the best in hockey history. He played a crucial role in India's bronze medal wins at the 2020 and 2024 Olympics. Sreejesh also won the FIH Best Male Goalkeeper award for 2020-21 and was part of gold-winning teams at the 2014 and 2022 Asian Games.
