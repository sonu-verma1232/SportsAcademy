Sunil Chhetri

He is a professional Indian footballer who plays for the Indian Super League club Bengaluru. He is the all-time scorer in the history of the India Super League. His unique football skills are his goal-scoring abilities, leadership, link-up play and many more. Sunil Chhetri is the fourth-highest international goalscorer, most-capped player and all-time top goal scorer of the Indian National Team. With all these achievements, he is known as the greatest Indian football player ever. He helped India win the 2007, 2009, and 2012 Nehru Cup, the 2011, 2015, 2021, 2023 SAFF championships and many more. He also received the Arjuna Award in 2011, the Padma Shri Award in 2019, and the Khel Ratna Award in 2021.

PK Banerjee

PK Banerjee, or Pradip Kumar Banerjee, is another legendary and famous football player of India. He was an Indian professional footballer who played as a striker on India's National Football Team. He also captained the national team and, later on, became the coach of the national team. However, he has represented India in 52 official matches and scored 16 official goals for the country. He was the first to receive the Arjuna Award when the awards were instituted in 1961. He also won Padma Shri in 1990 and was named the Indian Footballer of the 20th Century by IFFHS. He was also awarded the FIFA Order of Merit.
